Wander Finds.

British model and cultural explorer Taloulah Mair is traveling the world — from Egypt to India to Pakistan — curating rare, handcrafted treasures. Her passion venture, Trip Loot, is a curated lifestyle collection of global finds — each piece a story, a memory, a connection to a place and its people.

What I Do

I travel the world to discover unique, handcrafted treasures from diverse cultures — from bustling markets to hidden artisan workshops. Through Trip Loot, I bring these rare finds to you, blending fashion, culture, and storytelling into a collection that connects you with the soul of each place.

Who I Am

I'm Taloulah Mair — a British model, storyteller, and cultural explorer. My work goes beyond fashion; I seek meaning in movement, connection in craft, and stories in every place I visit. Through my travels, I aim to celebrate culture, individuality, and the beauty of the handmade.

My Work

My work is a blend of fashion, travel, and storytelling. From modeling campaigns to curating handcrafted pieces across continents, I create experiences that connect people with culture and craftsmanship. Each item I share through Trip Loot reflects a journey — shaped by tradition, inspired by discovery.
